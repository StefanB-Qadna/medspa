## Design Context

### Users
Women aged 30-55 actively researching aesthetic treatments online. They're comparing providers, reading reviews, and evaluating credibility — often browsing on mobile during personal time (evenings, weekends). They're discerning, health-conscious, and looking for someone they can trust with their face and body. The decision to book is high-stakes and emotional.

### Brand Personality
**Warm, refined, clinical.** Trustworthy medical expertise wrapped in approachable warmth. Not cold or sterile, not fluffy or salesy. The key differentiator is Dr. Robledo's surgical background — the brand should communicate "this person knows what they're doing" without intimidation. Think: a beautifully designed doctor's office where you immediately feel at ease.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Tone**: Editorial luxury with medical credibility. Closer to a high-end wellness publication than a typical med spa site.
- **Theme**: Light (cream/linen). Warm neutrals with brass accents. The palette should feel like natural materials — linen, stone, gold.
- **Anti-references**: Neon-lit "Instagram clinic" aesthetics. Dark mode with glowing accents. Generic stock-photo-heavy med spa sites. Anything that looks like a coupon site or discount provider.
- **Typography**: Serif display (Cormorant Garamond) paired with geometric sans (Jost). Editorial hierarchy.
- **Photography**: Real clinic, real patients, real provider. No stock photos.

### Accessibility
Enhanced accessibility: WCAG AA minimum, with extra attention to larger touch targets, generous contrast ratios, reduced motion support, and screen reader semantics.

### Design Principles
1. **Trust through transparency** — Show the real space, real provider, real results. No vague promises.
2. **Quiet confidence** — Let quality speak. No hard sells, no urgency tactics, no "limited time" pressure.
3. **Editorial depth** — Treat content like a publication, not a brochure. Educate, don't just promote.
4. **Surgical precision in design** — Every detail intentional. Spacing, typography, color — nothing accidental.
5. **Warmth without softness** — Approachable but not weak. Warm but not saccharine. Professional but not cold.
